Talking Pigeon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Piegeon ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Talking Pigeon mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Microphone & Voice Recordings

Talking Pigeon uses your device's microphone to let you talk to the pigeon character. When you tap to record, the App captures a short audio clip, applies a voice effect, and plays it back to you.

These recordings are processed entirely on your device and are not uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on our servers. They are temporarily held in memory or local device storage only for as long as needed to play back the effect, and are discarded afterward. (If a future update changes this — for example, by adding cloud-based voice processing — this policy will be updated to reflect that before the change ships.)

You can deny or revoke microphone access at any time in your device settings. If you do, voice-based features of the App will not work, but you can still use the rest of the App.

Advertising Data

The App displays ads through AppLovin MAX, a mediation platform that may serve ads from AppLovin itself or from third-party ad networks in its mediation chain. To serve and measure ads, AppLovin and its partner networks may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as your advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate (coarse) location derived from it
  • Device model, operating system, and language settings
  • App usage and ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, install attribution)

This data is collected and processed by AppLovin and its network partners according to their own privacy policies, not by us directly. You can review AppLovin's privacy practices at applovin.com/privacy.

Information We Don't Collect

We do not require account creation, and the App does not collect your name, email address, or other directly identifying information. We do not use cloud save, analytics SDKs, or in-app purchases in this App.

2. How We Use Information

Any data described above is used solely to operate the App's voice playback feature and to display and measure advertising. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Children's Privacy

Talking Pigeon is designed for a general audience and may be used by players of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Because the App shows ads, we configure our ad settings to comply with applicable child-directed advertising rules (such as COPPA in the United States and the Google Play Families Policy), including limiting personalized ad targeting where required for child or mixed audiences.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can address it.

4. Data Retention

Voice recordings are not retained after playback, as described above. Advertising identifiers and related data are retained by AppLovin and its partners according to their own retention policies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App and the limited data it processes. However, no method of electronic processing or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights (EEA, UK, and Other Regions)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or another region with similar data protection laws, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and to object to or withdraw consent for certain processing (such as personalized advertising). Since most ad-related data is collected directly by AppLovin and its partners, these requests are often best directed to them via their own privacy tools, but you can also contact us and we will assist where we can.

7. International Data Transfers

Piegeon is based in Poland (EU). Advertising partners such as AppLovin may process data in other countries, including the United States, using appropriate safeguards as described in their own privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example as the App's features change. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
